TRADING ENTERPRISES

ABOUT US

Trading Enterprises was established in 1971 and have a long history of providing automotive expertise alongside caring and attentive service. We are very proud of our modern showrooms and cutting-edge facilities.

Dodge has a long history of producing muscle cars and has led the way in America with its pioneering spirit and advanced engineering. The manufacturer’s impressive past is filled with successes in NASCAR and production cars. At Trading Enterprises we share this pride in our heritage and have over forty years of experience in the automotive industry.

Every member of the team at each Trading Enterprises branch brings you expert care backed by our many years of experience in the automotive industry. They pass our experience on to you by providing assistance and helpful advice. Their wealth of knowledge makes it easy for you to select the right Dodge model for you.

Please get in touch to find out more about any of the Dodge vehicles or after-sales services we offer across our 12 locations.

CERTIFIED PRE-OWNED CARS

HOW CAN WE HELP YOU?

Buying a Certified Pre-Owned car from Trading Enterprises is one of the best ways to ensure incredible value for money as well as exceptional motoring adventures. We pride ourselves in stocking the widest range and best quality certified pre-owned vehicles.

Only vehicles under 4 years old, below 80,000 Kilometers and major accident free are eligible to become Dodge certified pre-owned vehicles.

Dodge certified pre-owned cars are subject to a 125 point check by our Dodge trained technicians to ensure quality and reliability giving you complete peace of mind.
